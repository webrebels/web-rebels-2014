What's with the price tag?

NodeSchool events are limited in size and we want to avoid people signing up but not showing up. The entire amount will be donated to the meetup groups helping us: BartJS, BergenJS and Framsia. Fronter, Web Rebels and Booster Conference does not recieve any funds from this event.
